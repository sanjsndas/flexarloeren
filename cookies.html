Our Policy on the Use of Cookies

Date of Effect: 06.24.2026

This document details the cookie practices employed by Flexarloeren. It describes how we utilize cookies and comparable digital tools to process information concerning your interactions with our website, all while adhering to relevant data protection statutes. We are committed to clarity regarding the acquisition, deployment, and retention of your personal details, recognizing the paramount importance of safeguarding your privacy.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are tiny data files that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you browse. Their primary function is to retain specific information about your visit, thereby optimizing and customizing your journey through the site. They are instrumental in recalling your configurations and choices, facilitating a more personalized interaction.

Categories of Cookies We Deploy

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are indispensable for the website's core operations and cannot be disabled within our infrastructure. They support fundamental processes such as navigating pages and securing access to restricted sections.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These tools enable us to identify and quantify website traffic and observe how users navigate our digital platform. This data is crucial for continuous enhancement of our website's operational efficiency.
  • Preference Cookies: Employed to enrich your experience by retaining your choices and individualized settings, these cookies facilitate the provision of customized functionalities.
  • Targeting/Marketing Cookies: Designed to present advertisements that align more closely with your profile and inclinations. They also serve to regulate ad frequency and evaluate the impact of marketing initiatives.

User Consent

Your continued engagement with Flexarloeren signifies your agreement to our deployment of cookies as described herein. While you retain the option to control or prevent cookies via your browser configurations, be aware that such actions may diminish the website's performance and your overall user experience.

Administering Your Cookie Preferences

The majority of internet browsers offer mechanisms to manage cookies directly through their settings. These controls empower you to either permit or reject cookie storage. For comprehensive guidance on identifying, handling, and removing cookies, please consult your browser's support documentation.

We wish to emphasize that opting to restrict or eliminate cookies could potentially impact the website's functionality and render certain services inaccessible.

Protection of Personal Information

We accord the utmost importance to securing your personal data and implement robust technological safeguards. Although absolute online security cannot be guaranteed, we are committed to upholding stringent security protocols to mitigate risks of unauthorized intrusion and data compromise.

Individual Data Rights

In compliance with relevant legislation, including but not limited to GDPR and CCPA, you possess the prerogative to inquire about the personal information we maintain concerning you, which encompasses data collected via cookies during your website usage. Furthermore, you are entitled to request the rectification, modification, or erasure of your personal details. To assert these rights, please utilize the designated contact methods available on our platform.

Revisions to Our Policy

This Cookie Policy may undergo periodic revisions to account for evolutions in our operational procedures, technological advancements, regulatory mandates, or other influencing elements. We commit to informing you of any material alterations through suitable communication avenues and advise you to consult this policy regularly for current details regarding our cookie management.
