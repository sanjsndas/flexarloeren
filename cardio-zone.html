Cardio zone

The Flexarloeren cardio zone is a workout format designed for those who prefer active and rhythmic workouts in a comfortable environment. It features modern cardio equipment, allowing you to find the right workout for any fitness level. The spacious training area is designed to allow visitors to exercise freely, choose a comfortable pace, and gradually add variety to their program.

Cardiovascular training is an essential part of regular physical activity. It easily combines with strength training, functional exercises, and other workouts, helping to enrich every visit to the gym. A variety of equipment allows you to vary the format, duration, and intensity of your workouts, keeping your workout engaging.

The well-thought-out organization of the space allows you to fully focus on movement and make each workout as comfortable as possible.

Modern equipment

The cardio zone is equipped with modern exercise equipment designed for various types of physical activity. Each visitor can choose the equipment that suits their preferences and allows them to organize their workout in the most convenient format.

The variety of exercise equipment opens up vast opportunities for creating a personalized program. During a single session, you can use several types of equipment, combining exercises and varying intensity. This approach makes the training process more interesting and avoids monotony.

All exercise machines feature a user-friendly design, intuitive controls, and undergo regular maintenance, ensuring comfortable conditions for daily exercise.

Individual pace of classes

One of the cardio zone's unique features is the ability to choose your own workout pace. Each user can determine the duration of their workout, adjust the speed of their exercises, and find the optimal workout mode to suit their preferences.

This approach makes the service universally suitable for both beginners and experienced exercisers. If needed, the program can be gradually made more challenging by increasing the duration of individual stages or combining different types of cardio.

The flexibility of the training process allows you to easily adapt classes to your personal schedule and physical abilities.

Variety of training programs

The cardio zone offers the opportunity to create a variety of workout options. Visitors can perform long, leisurely workouts or choose more dynamic circuits with varying speed and intensity.

Combining several types of cardio equipment allows you to add variety to your routine and make each workout unique. Varying the order of exercises helps maintain interest in your workouts and opens up new options for physical activity.

A regular variety of training complexes contributes to a comfortable and exciting training process.

Comfortable training space

The cardio area is designed with every user's comfort in mind. The spacious space allows for free movement between machines, and the clever placement of equipment ensures comfort even during peak hours.

Particular attention is paid to lighting, ventilation, and cleanliness. This creates favorable conditions for active movement and long workouts during classes.

The convenient organization of the space allows you to fully concentrate on your exercises and enjoy every visit.

Suitable for all skill levels

The cardio zone is suitable for people of all fitness levels. Regardless of experience, each participant can choose a comfortable workout duration and a suitable pace.

Beginners can gradually become familiar with the equipment's capabilities and master the basic principles of cardio training. More experienced users can combine various activities, creating rich and varied workout programs.

This format makes the cardio zone a universal space for regular exercise.

Possibility of combination with other trainings

Cardio workouts are easily combined with other forms of physical activity. A visit to the cardio zone can be included before strength training, after the main workout, or used as a standalone activity.

The flexibility of this approach allows for varied training and tailored to personal preferences. Thanks to a wide selection of equipment, each participant can choose their own exercise sequence and duration.

This versatility makes the service popular among visitors with different training habits.

Convenience for every visit
